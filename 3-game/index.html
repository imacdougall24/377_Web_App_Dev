Shut the Box!

Welcome to Shut the Box!

Shut the Box is a dice game that tests your luck against the odds.
Traditionally, Shut the Box is played inside a wooden box with numbered tiles
used to keep track of the numbers already rolled by a player.
In this version, you will be using a virtual "box" to roll dice and keep track of your rolls.

How to Play:

Roll the dice and try to roll every number between 2 and 12 without
repeating any of the previously rolled sums. If you get lucky, you'll win!
